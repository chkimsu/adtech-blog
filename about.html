About This Blog

Your resource for understanding the complex world of ad tech

Our Mission

The Ad Tech Blog is dedicated to demystifying the complex landscape of digital advertising technology. We provide in-depth, technical content for professionals working in programmatic advertising, real-time bidding, analytics, and privacy compliance.

Whether you're an engineer building ad tech systems, a data scientist optimizing campaigns, or a product manager navigating privacy regulations, you'll find valuable insights here.

What We Cover

  • Programmatic Advertising: The fundamentals and advanced concepts of automated ad buying
  • Real-Time Bidding (RTB): Deep technical dives into auction mechanics and optimization
  • Ad Analytics & Attribution: Measurement strategies, attribution models, and data analysis
  • Privacy & Compliance: Understanding GDPR, CCPA, and the evolving privacy landscape
  • Industry Trends: Emerging technologies and the future of digital advertising

Why This Blog?

The ad tech industry moves fast, with constant evolution in technologies, regulations, and best practices. This blog aims to:

  • Provide technical depth often missing from surface-level content
  • Explain complex concepts with clear examples and code samples
  • Keep you updated on industry changes and their implications
  • Offer practical insights you can apply to your work
  • Foster knowledge sharing within the ad tech community

Built With

This blog is built with modern web technologies and hosted on GitHub Pages:

  • HTML5 & Semantic Markup: For structure and SEO
  • CSS3 with CSS Variables: Modern, responsive design with dark/light themes
  • Vanilla JavaScript: No frameworks, fast and lightweight
  • GitHub Pages: Free, reliable hosting
  • Utterances: Privacy-friendly GitHub-based commenting system

The entire source code is available on GitHub. Feel free to fork it, learn from it, or use it as a template for your own blog!
